Scanning vs. Lab Interfaces

Scanning lab results into Clicktate® is a great way to cut down on paper and storage, but there are a few disadvantages:

  • It's time-consuming
  • You are only able to view the results as an image; i.e. because the system isn't storing the numeric values of individual tests, it can't track changes over time or help indicate which values are out of range.

A better solution is to receive these lab results electronically. Instead of faxes, results will be delivered directly into your Clicktate® lab inbox, where they are easily matched to patients and posted into their chart in a format that Clicktate® can use to help you track and report.

How Do I Receive Lab Results Electronically?

It is surprisingly easy to get a lab interface into Clicktate®, and it starts with you contacting your lab representative.

  1. Contact your laboratory representative for each lab that you use
  2. Tell them you would like a lab interface with Clicktate®
  3. Ask them to contact Clicktate® at 888-825-4258 ext 801
  4. Let us know (by email or phone) the name of the lab and contact information of your representative

It's that easy, and usually your lab will completely cover the cost of building the interface. Depending on the specifications, we can often have your lab interface up and running in a very short time.
